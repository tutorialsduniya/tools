Body mass index (BMI) is a value derived from the mass (weight) and height of a person.

BMI Formula: BMI is defined as the body mass divided by the square of the body height. It's universally expressed in units of kg/m2.

A high BMI can be an indicator of high body fatness. BMI can be used to screen for weight categories that may lead to health problems but it is not diagnostic of body fatness or health of an individual.

Table for BMI categories
