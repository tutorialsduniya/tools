What is a YouTube Thumbnail ?

YouTube Video thumbnails let viewers see a quick snapshot of your video as they're browsing YouTube.

Generally, YouTube generates the thumbnail automatically for your video but if you don't like auto generated thumbnail then you can upload your custom thumbnail also.

The ideal ratio of your thumbnail should be 16:9 as it is the standard for most of the YouTube previews and the size of a thumbnail should not exceed above from 2MB.
