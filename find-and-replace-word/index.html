About our Find and Replace Word Tool

Our Find and Replace Word Tool is a simple free online tool that will help you to easily find multiple words and replace them with some other words in your text you wish to replace with.

How to use our Find and Replace Word Tool ?

  • First of all, write or paste your original text in the first box.
  • In the next box, write the word which you want to replace from your text.
  • Then, write the word which you want to insert in your text.
  • By default, our tool is case-insensitive. If you want to use case sensitivity, just click on the "Match Case" button.
  • Now, in the last box you can get your new text.
