Best tips to generate strong random password

  1. Always use a unique password for each account.
  2. Avoid using any personally identifiable information in your passwords. Names, birthdays, addresses etc are easily found online.
  3. Use passwords which are atleast 12 characters long and contain letters, numbers and special characters in it.
  4. Avoid weak or commonly used passwords like abc123, password, qwerty98765, temp101 etc. Some examples of strong passwords include: {Gb2IqJnElX@, 3f7vo1e}*x68, wRfL*mRnclJB etc.
  5. Never share your passwords via email or text message with anyone.
  6. Change your passwords when you have reason to, such as after you've shared them with someone or after a website has had a breach or if it's been over a year since you last changed it.
