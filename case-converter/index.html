About our Case Converter Tool

Our Case Converter Tool is a simple free online tool that will convert any text to UPPER CASE, lower case, Sentence case and Title Case.

UPPER CASE: It will transform all lower case letters into upper case and keeps upper case letters as upper case letters.

lower case: It will transform all upper case letters into lower case and keeps lower case letters as lower case letters.

Sentence case: It will transform the very first letter of first word in each sentence into Upper case.

Title Case: It will transform the first letter of all the words into Upper case.
